2022 · Hanoi

Joined Socotec Vietnam as the first BIM engineer.

July 2025 · Italy

First trip to Europe.

Avellino and Ferrara for work. Then Rome, Pisa, Florence, the Dolomites, Lake Garda for everything else. The Geisler Alm is the kind of place that changes how you talk about mountains afterward.
