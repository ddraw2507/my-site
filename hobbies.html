Good Ways to Spend an Afternoon

Hiking

Preferably somewhere with elevation and few people. The Dolomites set the bar impossibly high; since then I've been rediscovering the smaller routes closer to home, which turn out to be enough.

Karaoke

Loudly, with friends, off-key, and unapologetically. A counterweight to every other quiet thing on this page.

Personal finance

A long, unglamorous interest in the accumulate-slowly tradition. I keep an eye on Hòa Phát (HPG) and a spreadsheet that I take too seriously. The best financial decisions I've made have been boring ones, made early.

Photography, sometimes

Mostly with an iPhone, occasionally with an Insta360 Flow 2 Pro for walks and trips. I am not a good photographer. I am a patient one.
