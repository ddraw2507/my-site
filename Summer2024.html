Travelling alone for two weeks

Some trips are planned. Others are earned. This one felt like the second kind - a gift to myself for a year that asked more of me than I expected to give. I spent the final eight days of 2025 and the first days of 2026 in Taipei, and I came home with a suitcase of snacks, a camera full of thousands of photos, a notebook stamped at every MRT station I passed through, and a heart that felt a little lighter than when I left.
