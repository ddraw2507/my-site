Notes to Myself

Short essays and fragments. This page can work one of two ways: as a single long flow of short thoughts (like a commonplace book), or as an index of separate essays. For now, the commonplace-book pattern — a few short pieces separated by ornament dividers.

On building incrementally

The largest things I've been part of at work were built the same way: by not stopping. A plugin here, a template there, a better checklist, a sharper question. Grand gestures are fun to announce and easy to abandon. Small ones stack.

The hardest part of a long road is not starting it, and not finishing it. It's the quiet Wednesday in the middle, when nothing feels like progress and no one is watching.

On peace over drama

I've come to prefer the conversation that leaves no residue. The meeting that ends on time. The disagreement that ends with I see what you mean and not with a grudge carried home.
