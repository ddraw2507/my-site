July 2025 · Avellino & Ferrara

The working half of the trip.

Two weeks in two Italian cities I hadn't heard of a year before. Avellino is hill-country, quiet, the kind of place where the best restaurant is also somebody's living room. Ferrara is cycling-flat and honey-colored, with arcades that run for kilometers.

July 2025 · Rome, Pisa, Florence

Three days, three cities — a mistake I'd make again.

Rome is loud and hot and worth it. Pisa is a brief, good detour. Florence is the one I'd go back to for longer — the Uffizi in the morning, a slow afternoon walk along the Arno, then any trattoria whose menu is handwritten.

July 2025 · Dolomites & Lake Garda

The mountains that rearranged something.

The Geisler group from Alm path: a wall of pale limestone teeth against a sky so clear it looked unreasonable. Then south to Lake Garda, which is calmer and easier and exactly what was needed after the thin air.
