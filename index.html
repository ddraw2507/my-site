iii.

The Work

I lead a small BIM team in Hanoi, serving clients across Europe - Italy mostly, but also Germany, France, Spain, the UK. Six years into the construction industry, I’ve come to see two very different versions of myself: the first three years and everything that followed. Back then, I was all drive-weekends at the office, learning relentlessly, giving without expecting anything in return. But the next three years reshaped how I think. I stopped spending time on what doesn’t matter. Time became my most valuable asset. And the goal? Learn something new, every single day. What used to take me eight hours now takes four. Not because I do less but because I’ve learned to work faster, smarter, and more reliably, automating the repetitive to focus on what truly creates value. The other four hours? That’s where I invest in myself-learning, reading, exploring, or simply recharging. It sounds simple. It wasn’t.
